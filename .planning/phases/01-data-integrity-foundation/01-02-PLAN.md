<threat_model>
ASVS level: 1
Block on: high severity

- **TM-01: Misleading health-adjacent data.** Incorrect daily allocation can mislead the user's retainer habit tracking. Mitigation: centralize allocation in one helper and reuse it for daily totals, streaks, and charts.
- **TM-02: Data loss at midnight.** Splitting a running session into two stored records could lose the user's continuous log intent. Mitigation: keep one `Session(start:end:)` record and split only computed totals.
- **TM-03: Date/time edge cases.** Calendar boundaries can vary by locale and DST. Mitigation: use `Calendar.current.startOfDay(for:)` and calendar date arithmetic instead of fixed 24-hour assumptions.
</threat_model>
